Amity International School

After finishing my Bachelor of Science at Georgia Tech, I came to Massachusetts Institute to pursue my graduate studies. I was excited after Visitation Weekend, where I saw the inquisitiveness of my fellow students and all the different opportunities that this school had to offer. I choose Dr. Tian as my advisor and joined the Sloan Automotive Lab.

Moreover, I wanted to continue to be a part of student teams, since I love the varied experiences that they provide. This led me to join the MIT Hyperloop team. I also made the decision to be more active and became a part of many different intramural teams including soccer, badminton and Uni-Hoc.

As part of the curriculum, all students have to take 6 classes during the course of their Masters studies. I have taken the following till now:

  • Fluids (Fall 2015)
  • Computational Science and Engineering (Fall 2015)
  • Internal Combustion Engines (Spring 2016)
  • Dynamics (Fall 2016)
  • Thermodynamics (Fall 2016)
  • Entrepreneurship in Engineering: A Founder's Journey (Fall 2016, Listener)
